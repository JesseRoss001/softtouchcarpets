Cookie Policy

Last updated: 28 July 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve site performance.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies – enable basic site functions such as page navigation.
  • Analytics cookies – anonymously collect data on how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functionality cookies – remember choices you make, such as form field values.

3. Managing cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may affect some site features.
